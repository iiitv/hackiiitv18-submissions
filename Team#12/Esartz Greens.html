About Esartz GREENS


Esartz Greens is built for efficiency in waste disposal and pollution check. These are very common problems in today’s world and the major issue is unavailability of resources to handle these issues. Many people want to recycle waste, but are forced to throw it as they are unable to find a convenient means to recycle. Our aim is to connect such people to places that can help both parties, and most importantly the environment. Also, when visiting a new place, you can look for level of pollution and other factors about the quality of air in that area.
